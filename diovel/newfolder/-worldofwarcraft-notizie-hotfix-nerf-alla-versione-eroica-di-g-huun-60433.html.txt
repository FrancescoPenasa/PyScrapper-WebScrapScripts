now targets 1 player (was 3) on Heroic Difficulty.</li>		</ul></li>	</ul></li>	<li><strong>Siege of Boralus</strong>	<ul><li>Horde players who have not unlocked the dungeon should not receive a Seige completion.</li>	</ul></li></ul><p><strong>Items</strong></p><ul><li>Players who received Khor, Hammer of the Guardian before it was hotfixed to an item level befitting a transmog item have had their Your Sleeve (Rogue) damage significantly increased.</li>		<li>Blade in the Shadows (Rogue) damage provided slightly reduced.</li>		<li>Indomitable Justice (Paladin) damage increased.</li>		<li>Night's increase damage by 40% when engaged in combat with enemy players (was 60%).</li>			<li>Rune of Power increases spell damage by 30% when engaged in combat with enemy players (was 40%).</li>		</ul></li>		<li><strong>Frost</strong>		<ul><li>Glacial Spike damage reduced by 20% when attacking enemy players.</li>			<li>Concentrated Coolness (PvP Talent) increases Frozen Orb damage by 10% (was 30%).</li>		</ul></li>	</ul></li>	<li><strong><font color='#00c77b'>Monk</font></strong>	<ul><li><strong>Windwalker</strong>		<ul><li>Meridian Strikes (Azerite Trait) effectiveness reduced by 60% when when engaged in combat with enemy players.</li>		</ul></li>	</ul></li>	<li><strong><font color='#FFF569'>Rogue</font></strong>	<ul><li>Sharpened Blades (Azerite Trait) effectiveness reduced by 65% when when engaged in combat with enemy players.</li>		<li><strong>Outlaw</strong>		<ul><li>Deadshot (Azerite Trait) effectiveness reduced by 45% when engaged in combat with enemy players.</li>			<li>Turn the Tables increases damage by 10% (was 15%).</li>			<li>Dispatch damage reduced by 15% when engaged in combat with enemy players.</li>			<li>Killing Spree damage reduced by 20% when engaged in combat with enemy players.</li>			<li>Between the Eyes damage reduced by 20% when engaged in combat with enemy players.</li>		</ul></li>		<li><strong>Subtlety</strong>		<ul><li>Eviscerate damage reduced by 15% when engaged in combat with enemy players.</li>			<li>Phantom Assassin (PvP Talent) increases critical strike by 10% (was 35%).</li>			<li>Shadowstrike damage reduced by 10% when engaged in combat with enemy players.</li>		</ul></li>	</ul></li></ul><p><strong>Professions</strong></p><ul><li><strong>Archaeology</strong>	<ul><li>Arathi Highlands archaeology digsites are not available for players who are in the Warfront version of the zone. Speak to Zidormi to time travel to the previous version of Arathi Highlands.</li>	</ul></li>	<li><strong>Alchemy</strong>	<ul><li>Battle for Azeroth alchemy potions are now correctly classified as potions when searched on the Auction House.</li>	</ul></li>	<li><strong>Inscription</strong>	<ul><li>Fixed a bug that prevented Uldir Vantus Runes from functioning as expected.</li>	</ul></li></ul><p><strong>Quests</strong></p><ul><li>"Confrontation at the Black Temple" can once again be completed.</li></ul><p><strong>World Quests</strong></p><ul><li>Fixed a bug causing Crimson Gnarlvine to go missing on "Kiro's Desert Flower".</li>	<li>Fixed a bug that improperly prevented completion of some World Quests while the player was dead.</li></ul></div></blockquote><!-- blizz=cb50de --><br/><!-- blizz=7554a1 --><blockquote class="blizzpost"><cite class="header"><span class="blue_quoted"><img src="http://static.battlecraft.it/blizz.gif"> <a href="https://us.battle.net/forums/en/wow/topic/20768906803#1" class="postlink" rel="nofollow" onclick="this.target='_blank';this.target='_blank';">Arempy</a> ha scritto</span></cite><div class="body"><p>Update: These are now live.</p><p>We have the following PvP hotfixes to Arms Warriors and Beast Mastery Hunters currently in test, and will be applied to live servers shortly:</p><p><strong><font color='#C79C6E'>Warrior -
